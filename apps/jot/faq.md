### Does JOT collect any data?

No. JOT stores your focus text locally and does not transmit or collect personal data.


### Does JOT require an internet connection?

No. JOT works offline and does not make network requests related to user data.


### Where is my data stored?

JOT uses standard macOS local storage, so your data stays on your device.


### How do I report a bug?

Send an email to [support@nullbytes.io](mailto:support@nullbytes.io) with your macOS version, JOT version, and a short description of the issue.


### Will there be an iCloud sync option?

Not currently. If that changes, the privacy policy will be updated.
